About me

I am a PhD candidate in the Department of Mathematics, Statistics, and Computer Science at University of Illinois at Chicago. My research interests are random polynomials, random matrices, PDEs, SPDEs, fluid dynamics, harmonic analysis, high-dimensional probability/statistics, Nonlinear Schrödinger Equations and Whitney-type extension problems. I like problems combining Probability, PDEs, Harmonic Analysis, Mathematical Physics, Spectral Theory and all kinds of applications.

For the academic year 2024-2025, I am a visiting scholar at the Department of Mathematics, Michigan State University.
